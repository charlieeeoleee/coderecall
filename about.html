About the Platform

What is Code Recall?

Code Recall is a gamified learning platform designed to support Computer System Servicing students through interactive lessons, quizzes, XP rewards, badges, and guided progression.

🎯

Our Mission

To make technical learning more engaging, motivating, and easier to understand through gamified experiences that encourage students to continue learning and practicing.

🌟

Our Vision

To become an innovative learning support system that helps students build stronger knowledge and confidence in CSS-related subjects through interactive and rewarding digital learning.

Why Code Recall Matters

Built to answer the needs of learners who want a more engaging way to study.

🧠 Better Understanding

Technical concepts can be difficult, so the platform breaks them down into more manageable learning experiences.

🎮 Higher Engagement

XP, badges, and progress tracking make students feel more involved and motivated while learning.

📘 Guided Progression

Learners move step by step through lessons, assessments, and rewards, helping them stay focused and organized.

Focus Areas

The platform currently supports the following subject areas.

🖥

Computer Hardware

Covers computer parts, their functions, proper handling, and basic troubleshooting.

⚡

Electrical Wiring and Electronics Circuit Components

Covers electricity, safety, wiring concepts, and electronics circuit components related to CSS learning.
